About

My name is Kaihua Tang. I'm a master student major in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I also have achieved an additional master degree at Graduate School of IPS (Waseda University) through a dual master programme.
I got my Bachelor's degree from IEEE honor class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. During my sophomore summer break (2013), I took a summer session at UC Berkeley. In my junior year (2014), I decided to apply a two-year exchange programme to Waseda University. Fortunately, I was honored to be selected and spend the next two years in Japan, and got an additional Engineering degree from Waseda. From Aug 2016 up to now, I continue my study at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
I'm mainly interested in computer vision and machine learning. My previous research field is cross-age face recognition and age estimation. Recently, I decide to switch my reseach topic to semantic segmentation and visual reasoning.

Image Inpainting (Toshiba Intern)
During my internship in Research Center of Toshiba, my subject was scenery image inpainting. The company wanted to develop a inpainting system that automatically generate the missing part of an image. The system was intended to help users piecing scenery images together or removing some objects/people from a scenery photo. I implemented a patch-based inpainting method with weighted explicit boundary.
