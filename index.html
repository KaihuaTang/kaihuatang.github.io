Image Inpainting (Toshiba Summer Intern)
During my internship in Toshiba, my subject was scenery image inpainting. The group wanted to develop a inpainting system that automatically generate the missing part of an image. The system was intended to help users piecing scenery images together or removing some objects/people from a scenery photo. I implemented a patch-based inpainting method with boundary enhancement.
